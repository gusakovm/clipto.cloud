Integrations

June 8, 2023

Integrations are a cool feature for those who frequently use Telegram for work. Currently, Clipto supports two integrations: Zoom and Calendly. Let's connect your Zoom account so you could create a meeting link in just two clicks! 🚀

  1. In the main Clipto menu → "Install App" → "Zoom"

  2. Authorize your Zoom account.

Great! Your Zoom account is now connected to Clipto! There are several ways to create meetings 👇

  1. In any chat, type @clipto_bot + "space" → select Zoom from the list.

  2. In the Clipto chat, click "Quick Actions" and select "Create Zoom Link". Share this link with all meeting participants.

  3. In the main Clipto menu, in the "Apps" section, click on Zoom. Create an instant link or schedule a recurring meeting.

Inline Mode

May 10, 2023

Finally, we can tell you about another key feature of Clipto - Inline Mode! It allows you to instantly share all your saved Clipto notes right in your Telegram chat!

  1. In any chat, type @clipto_bot and press "space".

  2. Start typing the name of your clip, and Clipto will display all the matching clips.

  3. Click on the clip to send it. This way you can send clips in any of your chats!

Now, in just two clicks, you can easily share any information: interesting posts, documents, files, and notes!

With Inline Mode, we are one step closer to our mission of increasing the efficiency of 100,000 people by 10x! But we have another super important feature coming up very soon, and you'll be the first to know! 🔥

Clips & Folders

April 1, 2023

For us, Clipto is synonymous with efficiency. And we are excited to introduce a tool that allows you to conveniently save and organize all the content passing through us in Telegram. And we named it 🥁 drumroll, please 🥁 FOLDERS (original, we know).

By clicking the "Menu" button, we navigate to the main Clipto interface. Here, all the folders with your clips will be displayed.

Let's create your first clip (note) and save a Telegram post.

  1. Select any message or post from your chats.

  2. Forward that message to Clipto.

  3. Follow the instructions: give the clip a name, choose your desired folder to save it or create a new one.

Congratulations, you have saved your first clip! 🔒
