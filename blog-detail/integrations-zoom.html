Zoom: Create a meeting in just a few clicks

Jun 12, 2023

Integrations are a cool feature for those who frequently use Telegram for work. One of the most popular integrations among our users is, of course, Zoom. It allows you to create a Zoom meeting in Clipto with just two clicks and send the link to your colleagues so that you can start discussing what's important right away!

Connecting your Zoom account:

  1. In the main Clipto menu, click "Show All" at the bottom of the screen and select "Install App" → "Zoom."

  2. In the new window, enter your Zoom username/password or sign in through Google/Apple/Facebook.

Great! Your Zoom account is now connected to Clipto!

Creating a Zoom meeting in Clipto:

There are three ways to create meetings in Zoom:

  1. In any chat, type @Clipto_bot + "Space", start typing "Zoom," and click on Zoom in the dropdown window. Clipto will instantly generate a meeting link.

  2. In the Clipto chat, click "Quick Actions" and select "Create Zoom Link". Share this link with all meeting participants.

  3. In the main Clipto menu, in the "Apps" section, click on Zoom. Create an instant link or schedule a recurring meeting.
